📱 About the App

HiveLife is a convenient assistant for finding restaurants, cafes, shops, parking lots, and attractions near you. The app uses Apple Maps data and is completely free.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How does place search work?

The app shows places within a selected radius from your current location. You can filter results by category.

Why don't I see some places?

Apple Maps may not display all places in certain areas. Try increasing the search radius or selecting a different category.

How do I add a place to favorites?

Tap the heart icon next to the place name. All favorite places are saved in your profile.

How do I build a route?

Tap on any place, then select "Build Route". The app will open the route in Apple Maps.

Is the app paid?

No, HiveLife is completely free and does not require registration.

What data does the app collect?

The app only requests your current location to find places near you. This data is not shared with third parties.

Why don't the discounts match reality?

The app generates fake discounts to demonstrate potential functionality that may be implemented in the future.
